Reservations

The camp summer season is nine weeks long, and there are 4 fall weekends. The maximum stay is normally two weeks; an optional third week may be requested after all two-week reservations have been accommodated. In addition, there are a few off-season programs, including a music camp and 2 yoga retreats.

No children under the age of four are permitted, and no pets.

Lottery

Three Mile Island is very popular, and reservations fill up early. All reservations postmarked between January 1 and February 1 are entered into a lottery. Reservations postmarked after February 1 are welcome, but they will only be considered after all those postmarked between January 1 and February 1 have been processed.

Fall Reservations

The Fall season includes four weekends. You may apply for as many Fall Weekends as you like, whether or not you have also applied for a Summer reservation. Getting Here

Guests are ferried from Shep Brown's Boat Basin in Meredith, NH, in the Appy, which leaves Shep's daily at 9am, 12 noon, and 5pm. On Saturday, the typical arrival day, there is also a 2pm launch.
